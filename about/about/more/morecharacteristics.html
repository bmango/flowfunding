More Characteristics of Groups Flow Funders Like to Support

 

  • Grassroots orientation
  • Spiritually-engaged activism
  • Healing people’s relation to place—this can include environmental, environmental justices, and human rights issues
  • Promotes cross-cultural understanding
  • Promotes ethical visions for the future

      - Elias and Elizabeth

 

Groups that:

  • Understand the systemic nature of the seemingly diverse problems which the world now faces, and thus deal with ‘root causes, rather than symptoms
  • Respect community-based and earth-based wisdom
  • Synthesize heart and mind, art and science, the personal/spiritual and the global/political
  • Recognize the importance of both cultural and ecological diversity.
  • Have the courage, where necessary, to be different

      - Helena
