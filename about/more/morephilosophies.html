More Philanthropy Philosophies

 

“I’m a responsible and proud part of a big circle of giving. I trust that a person can make further steps.”
      - Aigul

“You cannot be rich among poor people. I hope after the gift their quality of life will change for the better. They will give a gift to someone else.  If not me, then who will do this?”
      - Tamara

“I want to develop philanthropy in our region. I want to create a milieu of local business giving and a structure to do that.”
      - Makhmut

“I want the gift to be useful. I teach people to use the gifts to overcome difficulty in their lives. I want to decrease the amount of people not willing to change themselves.”
      - Sonia

“This is a gift from God.  I participate and you trust me. When I make a gift I think whether it will help the recipient to help others. I think whether we can attract other funds also.  I choose members of a group who are responsible.”
      - Nilzia

“This is my first year so I’m still figuring out my philosophy. What comes to me is that the life surrounding one should be getting better.”
      - Vladimir
