More Advice from Flow Funders

 

“Have fun.  This is a joy.  Let your heart lead you.  Watch for what the angels put in your path.” 
      - Justine and Michael Toms

“1) Take your time to develop your giving philosophy.  After giving a few grants you will know which grants have the impact you want.
2) Use your intuition.  If it feels right in your gut, it’s a good idea. Do not fund it otherwise.”
3) Tell people you have a friend with some money, or you might be able to get some money, rather than being known as a rich person.”
      - Enid Schreibman

Trust your intuition and don’t hesitate when you feel something is good and worthwhile. It might not always work out the way you anticipated, but at least you gave the recipients a chance.
      - Lilian
